Robotic and Coding I (6-9 years old)


No.
Lesson Topic


Key Objective

1 Glowing Snail
- Build a LEGO model
- UConnect the model and device
- Program the Smart hub LED to show a flash of light

2 Cooling Fan

- Build a LEGO model
- Connect the model and device
- Program the motor to turn at different speeds

3 Moving Satellite
- Build a LEGO model
- Connect the model and device
- Program the motor to turn for a set amount of time
- Program the motor to turn the other way

4 Spy Robot
- Build a LEGO model
- Connect the model and device
- Program the Motion Sensor to detect motion

5 The Science Rover
- Explore different ways scientists and engineers reach remote places.
- Create and program Milo the Science Rover.
- Document how Milo can help you discover a special plant specimen.

6 Motion Sensor
- Create and program Milo’s object-detector arm using the Motion Sensor Input.
- Document how Milo can help you discover a special plant specimen.

7 Milo’s Tilt Sensor
- Create and program Milo’s messaging arm using the Tilt Sensor.
- Document Milo’s communication with the base.
8 Collaborating
- Create and program a device to move the plant sample.
- Document and present how Milo's mission went overall.

9 Pulling
- Explore what forces are and how they can make objects move.
- Create and program a robot to investigate the effects of balanced and unbalanced forces on the motion of an object.
- Document and present findings about forces.
10 Speed
- Explore race car features.
- Create and program a race car to investigate what factors would make it go faster.
- Document and present ways to make your car go the fastest.
11 Robust Structures
- Investigating what characteristics of a building would help make it resistant to an earthquake
- Using an earthquake simulator from LEGO bricks
12 Frog’s Metamorphosis
- Explore what you know about the stages in the life cycle of a frog, from birth to adult.
- Document the changing characteristics of your model throughout different stages of a frog’s life.
13 Plants and Pollinators
- Explore how different organisms take an active role in plant reproduction.
- Create and program a model of a bee and flower to mimic the relationship between the pollinator and the plant.
