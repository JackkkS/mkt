Screenless Programming (2-5 years old)


No.
Lesson Topic


Key Objective

1 Explore the Café
- Explore the components of the Café’ set
- Begin counting as they build a menu item

2 Birthday Celebrations
- Explore the concept of parts of a whole
- Count and practice one-to-one correspondence
3 Food Patterns
- Sort by shape, size, and color
- Practice making patterns
- Recognize patterns

4 Double Up!
- Learn about doubling quantity
- Practice counting and recognizing sets of numbers
- Compare two or more objects

5 Café’ Bingo
- Match shapes by one-to-one correspondence
- Explore 2D and 3D objects
- Follow the sequence of events while playing a game

6 Let’s Go Shopping
- Build a variety of items.
- Sort by multiple characteristics
- Explore price and money

7 Trading Bricks
- Match items by one-to-one correspondence
- Count and recognize the number of objects in a set
- Explore trade and money
- Follow the sequence of events while playing a game

8 Welcome to STEAM Park
- Become familiar with the set
- Build models using the in-box building inspiration cards
- Meet the characters in the STEAM Park
- Explore the imaginary STEAM Park setting

9 Ramps
- Observe what happens when they place objects on a ramp
- Make predictions
- Measure how far objects move
- Record data using graph
10 Moving on Water
- Experiment with the idea of sinking or floating
- Learn which sail design works best for the boats in the set
- Record data using graphs
11 Probability
- Practice making predictions
- Record data using graph or charts
12 Performing Arts
-Learn about different kinds of performances
- Create their own act for a show
- Present or role-play their act
13 Gears
- Interlock gears
- Make the gears turn
14 Chain Reaction
- Identify cause and effect
- Create their own chain reactions
15 Wheeling away
- Explore the components of the Tech Machines set
- Learn about the function of wheels
- Recognize shapes and attributes
- Use spatial awareness to understand how wheeled vehicles move
16 Heavy load
- Explore the components of the Tech Machines set
- Explore weight and compare objects
- Use comparative language
- Design a vehicle to help with a task, and demonstrate how it works
17 Safety First
- Explore the components of the Tech Machines set
- Discuss safety
- Think creatively to solve safety problems
- Describe how safety elements, such as a wind shield, function
18 Special Machine Parts
- Use spatial awareness to understand how object move
- Demonstrate how to solve problems
- Observe and describe special parts and functions
- Build a model with a special part and demonstrate how it works
- Ask questions, make observations, and gather information to inform the design of machines
19 Machines with a Purpose
- Design machines or vehicles to solve problems
- Build a model and demonstrate how it works
- Demonstrate how tools help people to solve problems and accomplish tasks
20 Park Project
-Design machines or vehicles to solve problems
- Investigate how machines work together to complete a project
- Discuss sequence of events
- Explore roles related to projects
21 Help Machines
- Design machines or vehicles to solve problems
- Investigate how machines help people
- Test and adapt machine designs
- Work with others to build different machines
22 Water Vehicles
- Investigate water vehicles
- Compare types of vehicles
- Talk about the function of propellers and anchors
- Adapt their designs
23 First Trip
- Understand the function of action bricks
- Understand how to use the different types of bricks
