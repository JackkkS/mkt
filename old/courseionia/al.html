Animation Lab (Older than 14 years old)


No.
Lesson Topic


Key Objective

1 Storyboard
The student will learn how to animation making. Starting from writing a storyboard. Then, the student will learn about the importance of storyboarding and why should we make it before doing the animation.
2 Flipbook
The student will learn about the development of animation. Then, they will learn how to make their flip book. To understand the movement of animation.
3 Stop motion
The student will learn how to make stop motion, a widely used technique, which has the potential to make many interesting animations.
4 Character design (2D)
The student will learn the principle of designing animated characters (2D) and how to make them. In addition, they will know how to design characters which suitable for the story and easy to remember.
5 Character design (3D)
The student will learn how to make 3-dimension characters based on the designed characters in the previous lesson.

6 Comics
The student will learn how to make comics, which are the motivation for many famous animations. Moreover, they will know about important components that be a part of famous comics.
7 Make a move
The student will learn how to make a slide move. They will know how to make them able to smoothly move, such as walking, running, etc.

8 Music Video I
The student will develop creativity and planning skills through making music videos.
9 Music Video II
The student will develop creativity and planning skills through making music videos.
10 Final project I
The student will have an experience to do an animation from the beginning by using everything that they learned.
11 Final project II
The student will have an experience to do an animation from the beginning by using everything that they learned.
