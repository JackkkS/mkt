Robotic and Coding II (10-14 years old)


No.
Lesson Topic


Key Objective

1 Prevent Flooding
- Explore various ways that precipitation can change over seasons and how water can cause damage if it is not controlled.
- Create and program a floodgate to control the water level of a river.

2 Drop and Rescue
- Explore different weather-related hazards that can influence the survival of a population in your region.
- Create and program a device to relocate people and animals in a safe, easy-to-use, and respectful way or efficiently drop materials into an area.
3 Sort to Recycle
- Explore how better sorting methods for recycling can aid in cutting back the amount of waste that is discarded.
- Create and program a device that will sort recyclables according to their size and shape.

4 AI IDE Programming I
- Explore the different strategies animals use to catch their prey or to escape from their predators.
- Create and program a predator or prey in order to explore the relationship between them.

5 Animal Expression
- Explore different ways that animals communicate, including the unique methods of animals and insects that light up in the dark.
- Create and program an animal or insect to illustrate how it socially interacts with others of its species.

6 Extreme Habitats
- Explore different environments around the globe and across time, and describe what they might tell us about the lifestyle and success of a species.
- Create and program an animal or reptile that could have lived in a particular habitat.

7 Space Exploration
- Explore actual missions of rovers an imagine future possibilities.
- Create and program a space rover to achieve a specific task, such as: move in and out of a crater, collect a rock sample, drill a hole in the ground.

8 Hazard Alarm
- Explore different weather-related hazards that everyone needs to be aware of, like tsunamis, and hurricanes, and the warning systems.
- Create and program a device that could warn people to take action because dangerous weather is coming.

9 Cleaning the Ocean
- Explore why it is important to take care of the oceans and keep them clean of plastic debris.
- Create and program a device that can help physically collect plastics of certain types and sizes from the ocean.
10 Wildlife Crossing
- Explore the effect of road construction on animals and plants and imagine possibilities to reduce its impact.
- Create and program a device to allow animals to cross hazardous areas.
11 Moving Materials
- Explore different ways material is transported and assembled.
- Create and program a device that will help you carry and/or assemble differently sized objects, considering safety, efficiency, and storage.
12 Moon Base
- Explore why and how we could set up a base on the moon.
- Create and program a robot to move on the surface of the moon.
- Test your code to assemble the moon base at a specific location
13 Grabbing Objects
- Explore how prosthetic designs help people with disabilities.
- Create and program a prosthetic hand to move objects around.
- Test your program to make the hand as functional as possible
14 Send Messages
- Explore how messages can be transferred from one place to another.
- Create and program a joystick that will allow you to send messages by using codes
15 Volcano Alert
- Explore the different ways that scientists monitor volcanic activity.
- Create and program an alarm to indicate the different stages of volcanic activity
- Test your program and how well it indicates these different stages.
16 Inspection
- Explore how robots can be used to inspect narrow spaces.
- Create and program a robot to move on a surface and avoid obstacles.
- Test your program to make sure that it can detect walls, holes, a cave, and objects
17 Emotional Design
- Explore how robots can create positive emotions in everyday situations
- Build and program a robot that interacts with people in order to create positive emotions
18 City Safety
- Explore the different safety rules that are linked to city transportation and describe how some of them can be improved
- Create and program a device to improve city safety.
- Test your program and show how safety has been improved with your device
