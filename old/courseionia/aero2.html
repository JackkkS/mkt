Aerospace Engineering II (Older than 14 years old)


No.
Lesson Topic


Key Objective

1 Intro to Aerospace Engineering
In this lesson, main idea of Aerospace engineering will be described to students,
such as what is aerospace engineering? what engineer do?,what they need to know?
After that, the history of airplane development will be discussed.
2 Aircraft
In this lesson, students will learn about aircraft, such as what is aircraft, type of aircraft, and different between aircraft and airplane.

3 Part of Airplane
In this lesson, students will learn about compartment of airplane, and its duty.
4 Inspiration
In this lesson, students will learn about inspiration of inventor who build airplane, such as flying creature. Moreover, students will design their airplane following their inspiration

5 How to fly
In this lesson, students will learn about basic flight principle, and effect of airplane size on flying.

6 Force I
In this lesson, students will learn about force that action on airplane which can be used in basic flight mechanics.
7 Force II
In this lesson, students will learn about force that action on airplane which can be used in basic flight mechanics.
8 Force III
In this lesson, students will learn about force that action on airplane which can be used in basic flight mechanics.

9 Force IV
In this lesson, students will learn about force that action on airplane which can be used in basic flight mechanics.

10 Safety & Accident
The additional important for aircraft to fly is the safety system of aircraft.
The students will learn about aircraft’s safety system which consist of safety on board and engine.
In addition, they will know what they should do while in an accident.

11 How to become
In this lesson, students will learn about occupation which work about airplane, its duty, and the way to become.
12 Problem
In this lesson, students will combine everything that they know to creatively solve problem about Aerospace.
